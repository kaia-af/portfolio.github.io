  • This research project evaluates the effectiveness of using Virtual Reality (VR) in an electrical engineering lab, comparing the learning outcomes of circuit construction between VR settings with an avatar instructor and traditional lab environments. It aims to address the challenges faced by educational institutions during situations like the COVID-19 pandemic, by using VR to offer immersive and interactive learning experiences without the need for physical lab equipment. The study will assess variables such as student engagement, proficiency in building circuits, and perceptions of the AI-driven instructor, with the goal of enhancing educational methods and reducing costs.
